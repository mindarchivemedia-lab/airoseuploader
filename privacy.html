Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

AiroseUploader ("the Application"), operated by Mind Archive Lab, is a creator tool designed to help you manage and publish media. This policy explains how we handle your information when you connect your accounts across our supported platforms.

1. Third-Party Services We Use

To provide our core publishing features, AiroseUploader connects to official APIs from several third-party platforms. By using our application to authenticate with these services, you are also agreeing to their respective Privacy Policies:

  • TikTok: We use the TikTok API to facilitate video uploads.
  • YouTube: We use YouTube API Services. By connecting your YouTube account, you agree to the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Meta (Instagram & Facebook): We use the Graph API for publishing to Meta platforms.
  • Pinterest: We use the Pinterest API to allow publishing to your boards.

2. Information We Access

When you authorize AiroseUploader, we access only the data strictly necessary to fulfill your publishing requests. This may include:

  • Basic profile information (e.g., Username, Profile Picture) to identify your connected accounts.
  • Permissions to create, upload, and manage media content on your behalf, as explicitly authorized by you during the login process.

3. How Information is Used and Stored

We use this information solely to route your media to your chosen platforms. AiroseUploader does not store your videos, photos, or personal platform passwords on our servers. Authentication tokens are handled securely and used only for active publishing sessions. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any outside parties.

4. User Control & Data Deletion

You retain total control over your accounts. You can revoke AiroseUploader's access at any time by visiting the security or third-party app settings within your TikTok, Google/YouTube, Meta, or Pinterest accounts.

Data Deletion Request: If you want us to completely wipe any metadata, account linkages, or residual data associated with your AiroseUploader usage, please email us directly at mindarchive.media@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request." We will process your request within 7 business days.
