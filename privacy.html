Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Airose Publisher ("the Application") handles information when users connect their accounts and publish content.

Information We Access

The application may access basic account information and content that the user explicitly authorizes through the Pinterest authentication process.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only for the purpose of allowing users to publish their own media content to their Pinterest boards.

The application does not sell, distribute, or share user data with third parties.

Content Ownership

Users retain full ownership of all media and content uploaded through the application.

Data Storage

The application stores only the minimum data necessary to perform publishing actions requested by the user.

User Control

Users can revoke access to the application at any time through their Pinterest account settings.
